YIN Pitch Detection

A proper YIN-algorithm pitch tracker (FFT-accelerated autocorrelation) runs in a background worker to find every note with real onset detection — so repeated notes and rests come through correctly, not just one long blur.

Chord Detection

An optional polyphonic mode finds multiple simultaneous notes using iterative harmonic-salience analysis with spectral subtraction — built for piano and guitar chords, not just single melodic lines.

Gapless Preview

Your transcription is fully rendered to audio offline — piano-style harmonics plus subtle reverb — before playback ever unlocks. No buffering, no stutter, no silent failures.

Runs in Your Browser

Zero uploads, zero servers. Everything — decoding, analysis, MIDI export, and the audio preview — happens locally using the Web Audio API. Fast, private, free.

No Length Limits

Full songs, full albums — there's no 30-second cap. Longer files just take a little more analysis time, with live progress the whole way through.
